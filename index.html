Shark

Sharks cannot swim backwards

  • Scientific Name: Selachimorpha
  • Funniest Video: Funny Shark Video
  • Average life span: 20 to 30 years
  • Habitat: Sharks are found in every ocean on the planet.

Sharks are a powerful ocean predator with sleek movement, sharp senses, and a presence that commands respect. As apex predators, they play a vital role in maintaining the health of marine ecosystems by regulating prey populations. Their bodies are perfectly engineered for the deep, featuring a skeleton made of flexible cartilage and a highly developed sense of electroreception that allows them to detect the tiniest vibrations in the water. Far from being mindless hunters, these ancient creatures are essential architects of the ocean’s balance.
